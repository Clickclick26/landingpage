Live Host Academy

Hosts trained
for your brand.

The host is the single most important variable in a live commerce show. A great host can double your conversion rate. Our Academy exists to find, train, and deploy high-energy professional hosts who speak in your brand's voice — not a generic one.

01

Brand Voice Training

Every host we place goes through an intensive brief on your brand — its tone, its values, its audience, and its key selling points. When they go live, they feel like a natural extension of your team, not an outside hire.

02

High-Energy Selling Technique

Live commerce requires a specific set of skills — holding attention, creating urgency, handling technical glitches on screen, and converting curiosity into purchase. Our Academy trains hosts in all of it, so your shows consistently perform.

03

Platform-Specific Expertise

TikTok audiences behave differently from Amazon Live buyers or YouTube viewers. Our hosts are trained to adapt their style and energy to the platform they are broadcasting on — maximising engagement wherever your audience is watching.
